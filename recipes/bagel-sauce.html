Copycat McDonald's Bagel Sauce

Description

McDonald's breakfast bagel sauce is awesome, so why not put it on everything? This homemade copycat version of bagel sauce makes about a cup. Pour it into your own squeeze bottle and put it on everything!

Great for bagels, sandwiches, burgers, fries, fried chicken, all kinds of salads... Whatever you want.

Worth mentioning: it's super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 2 tbsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp steak sauce
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp dried dill
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
