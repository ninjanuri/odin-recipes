Potato Cheese Soup

Description

A cozy, rustic soup for cold-weather evenings. Perfect addition to your stew season rotation.

Gets better each time it's reheated!

Ingredients

  • 6-8 large baking potatoes (russets!)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 cups chicken broth or stock
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 8-12 strips cooked bacon (i use the entire package)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 8oz. shredded cheese (or more, if you want)
  • salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and cube the potatoes, then boil until soft.
  2. In a large pot or dutch oven, combine butter and flour over medium heat to make a roux. Cook 1 minute, then whisk in liquids.
  3. Add salt, pepper, potatoes, bacon and cheese. Stir well, then add sour cream at the last minute. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. To reheat, add milk.
